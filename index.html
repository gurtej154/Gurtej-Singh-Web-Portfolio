About me.

Aspiring future software developer from Adelaide, Australia.

I am a articulate individual with passion in developing ideas from scratch and seeing them thrive into something evolutionary.

During my junior years, I studied Architecture and attained Bachelor's of Applied Science from Curtin University and went on to being a graduate Architect, however during the short stint within the industry i realised that i lost my passion for the profession.

Currently I am enrolled within a Bootcamp for coding, challenging myself to learn new skills and pursue a career within the tech industry and hopefully utilise my software developer background within entrepreneurship and develop a tech-startup.
